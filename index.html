Manila Town Roleplay

Welcome to Manila Town Roleplay (MT-RP)
Established on July 10, 2025, Manila Town Roleplay was founded by Jzzy and developed by Peds with a vision to bring back the authentic experience of San Andreas Multiplayer.

MT-RP is a semi-med roleplay server designed for players who want to immerse themselves in a more realistic and story-driven environment. Every system, feature, and rule has been carefully crafted to strike a balance between realism and enjoyment, ensuring that roleplay remains at the heart of the community.

About The Server

At Manila Town Roleplay, our focus is on quality roleplay. Players are encouraged to create meaningful stories, engage in detailed character development, and interact with others in ways that bring the world of San Andreas to life.

We believe roleplay is more than just mechanics—it’s about creativity, storytelling, and community. That’s why we’ve built systems that enhance immersion without compromising the fun of the game.

Our Mission

To revive the spirit of SA-MP roleplay by creating a space where immersive storytelling and player interaction take center stage. Manila Town Roleplay is committed to building a lasting community where roleplay thrives.

How to Join Manila Town Roleplay

Follow these step-by-step instructions to connect to our SA-MP server.

For PC (Windows)

  • Download GTA San Andreas (PC Version)
    – You need the original GTA: San Andreas installed on your computer.
  • Install SA-MP Client
    – Download the SA-MP multiplayer client from the official website: https://www.sa-mp.mp/downloads.
    – Install it and link it to your GTA: San Andreas directory.
  • Add the Server
    – Open the SA-MP client.
    – Click the “Favorites” tab and add our server IP:
    play.manilatownrp.fun:7777
  • Join the Game
    – Double-click the server name in your favorites.
    – Enter your roleplay character name in the format:
    Firstname_Lastname

    – Click Connect and start your roleplay journey!

For Android

  • Download GTA: San Andreas (Android Version)
    – Available from the Google Play Store or other trusted sources.
  • Install SA-MP Mobile Client
    – Download a trusted SA-MP mobile client such as SA-MP Launcher from the Google Play Store.
  • Add the Server
    – Open the mobile SA-MP client.
    – Tap on Add Server and enter our server IP:
    play.manilatownrp.fun:7777
  • Join the Game
    – Select the server from the list.
    – Enter your character name in the format:
    Firstname_Lastname

    – Press Connect and enjoy roleplay on the go!

MTRP Server Rules

Welcome to Manila Town Roleplay (MTRP).
To maintain fair and immersive roleplay, all players are expected to follow the rules below. Violations may lead to warnings, imprisonment, strikes, or permanent bans depending on severity. Please read carefully before playing.

1. Character Rules

(Realistic RP, Powergaming, Metagaming, Godmodding, Backstories, RDM, Toxicity, OOC in IC, Death & Injuries, Impersonation)
Each rule has its own sanctions ranging from warnings to imprisonment and permanent bans depending on severity.

2. Gang Rules

  • Ranks: Only 1x R6 and limited R5s allowed.
  • Leadership Transfer: Must notify Gang Management.
  • HQ Raids: Require prior approval.
  • Warning/Strike System:
    • 3 Warnings = 1 Strike
    • 3 Strikes = Disband
  • Gang Hopping: Must wait 2 weeks before joining another gang.

3. Robbery Rules

Hold-Ups

  • Max 4 participants, all masked.
  • Only pistols and melee allowed.
  • Allowed loot: cash (50k–100k), medical/illegal items, weapons, repair kits.
  • Prohibited loot: cellphones, radios.

Bank Robbery

  • Max 6 participants.
  • Hostage required (max ransom \$100k).
  • High-class weapons allowed.
  • 5+ police must be on duty.
  • Must request admin to spectate.

Business Robbery

  • Max 4 participants.
  • Hostage optional.
  • Medium-class weapons only.
  • 3+ police must be on duty.

4. Roleplay Violations & Penalties

Light Offenses

  • Avoiding RP → 60 mins jail
  • Healing/Vesting in Gunfight (accidental) → Warning
  • Chicken Running / Bunny Hopping → 30 mins
  • Ninja Jacking → 30 mins
  • Car Surfing → 25 mins
  • Unrealistic Actions (e.g., dancing in gunfight) → 30 mins
  • Using Toys Non-RP → 25 mins
  • Changing Clothes to Evade → 30 mins
  • Spamming/Joke Misuse → 30 mins

Major Violations

  • Robbing Without Police → 30 mins
  • Deathmatching → 60 mins
  • Powergaming → 60 mins
  • Metagaming → 50 mins
  • Killing on Sight → 30 mins
  • Revenge Killing → 45 mins
  • Cop Baiting → 45 mins
  • Spawn Killing → 45 mins
  • Item Exploiting → 60 mins
  • CK Without OOC Consent → 60 mins
  • Robbing New Players → 30 mins
  • Green Zone Violation → 45 mins
  • Mask Rule Violation → 30 mins
  • Invalid Revenge RP → 45 mins

5. Point War Rules

  • Must wear /bandana and official gang skin.
  • Ground vehicles only (no flying).
  • All weapons allowed except M4.
  • No animation abuse.
  • Stay within turf boundaries.
  • LEOs may only respond if civilians report.
  • No alliances between gangs.
  • No revenge killing (RK).
  • Must have R5–R6 for takeover.

Punishments: Jail (30–120 mins), warnings, strikes, or suspensions depending on severity.

By playing on MTRP, you agree to follow all rules above.
Full Rules and Regulations are available in our Discord server.
